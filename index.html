Works

View a few of our past projects to get an idea of the kind of problems we like to tackle

Most of the projects listed below are exploritory endeavors we embarked on to test the limits of what's possible in healthcare

A.I. Tumor Segmentation

Applying A.I. to diagnose cancer faster and more affordably

By using open source datasets from various research institutions around the world, we have created algorithms that can segment brain tumors with similar accuracy to human radiologists but in a more scalable way.

Automated Tissue Segmentation

Bringing medical visualizations to a whole new level

By utilizing custom algorithms and pipelines, we have built a process to automatically segment tissue from MRI and CT scans and 3D print human anatomical models, allowing for improved case visualization and taking the first steps towards bioprinting.

Orthopedic Surgeon Aide

A visualization for pre-operational planning and implant shaping

One of the most exciting uses of 3D printing in the medical field today is for implant shaping and operation planning in orthopedics. By having a 1:1 scale model outside the body, the surgeon can shape implants beforehand, saving time and pain for the patient.
